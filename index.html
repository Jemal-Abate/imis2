    About

    Learn More About Harari IMIS

    The Harari Region Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) is a centralized platform designed to integrate and streamline the collection, analysis, and dissemination of data across various sectors in the region. Supported by UNFPA, the system enhances evidence-based decision-making and policy formulation.

    • Comprehensive data management for regional development.
    • Facilitating cross-sectoral collaboration and analysis.
    • Improving accessibility to key demographic and socio-economic data.

    With Harari IMIS, stakeholders can access real-time data to address regional challenges effectively and foster sustainable development initiatives.
